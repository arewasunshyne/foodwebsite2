Our Featured Dishes

Ikokore

Ikokore is a delectable yoruba delicacy featuring grated water yam, fish chunks, lots of beef, shrimps and kpomo which all simmered in a rich palm oil-based sauce.

Ekpang Nkukwo

It is made of freshly grated cocoyam,then wrapped in leafy greens and slowly simmered with various types of fresh or smoked meat,fish,periwinkles and red oil

Ofe Owerri

A legendary traditional soup, native to the Owerri people of Eastern Nigeria. It is usually prepared with four different vegetable leaves and lots of assorted meats and fish chunks.
