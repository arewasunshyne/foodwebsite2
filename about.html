Our Story

Founded in 2022, Foods By Dunni was born out of a passion for sharing the rich, flavorful, and diverse hommemae meals. We take the stress of cooking and market runs off you and we delivery ready made hommemade meals. We bring the very best of Nigerian food and traditional dishes right to your doorstep.

Our mission is to deliver the best of Meals, prepared with love and care, while promoting the rich culture and heritage of Nigeria.
